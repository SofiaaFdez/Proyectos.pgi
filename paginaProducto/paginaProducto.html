The purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the viewer, and the pleasant exercise of effort and ingennuity for the grower.

By contrast with other plant cultivation practices, bonsai is not intended for production of lood or for medicine. Instead, bonsai practice focuses on long-term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container.
